Detailed description

The Sony Alpha a7 IV sets a new standard for full-frame mirrorless cameras. Combining outstanding 33-megapixel image quality with cutting-edge BIONZ XR image processing, it delivers incredibly lifelike textures and stunning color accuracy. Whether you're shooting dynamic street photography or expansive landscapes, its extensive dynamic range of 15 stops ensures every detail is captured flawlessly from deep shadows to bright highlights.

Videographers will find the a7 IV indispensable, thanks to 10-bit 4:2:2 recording and 4K 60p capabilities. Sony's acclaimed autofocus system has been enhanced, providing Real-time Eye AF capable of tracking fast-moving subjects with unprecedented precision. Constructed with a weather-sealed magnesium alloy body, this camera is designed to withstand the demands of professional field work while remaining comfortable during long shoots.
